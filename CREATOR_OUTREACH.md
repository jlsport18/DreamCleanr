# DreamCleanr Creator Outreach

This is the lightweight outreach playbook for AI/dev creators. Keep it manual, targeted, and honest.

## Goal

- get DreamCleanr in front of AI/dev creators who already talk about overloaded local machines
- generate credible demos and honest feedback
- build interest without paid acquisition or fake scarcity

## Best-Fit Creator Profiles

- AI dev creators on X and YouTube
- indie hackers who work on Mac
- local-model and local-LLM creators
- dev-tool reviewers who cover Docker, coding tools, and workflow setup

## Outreach Rules

- keep it personal and short
- lead with the problem, not with a press-style intro
- do not ask for positive coverage
- do not imply the product has detectors that are still planned
- offer access, not pressure

## DM Scripts

### Version 1: Direct

> Hey [Name], built a trust-first cleanup tool for AI/dev Macs that helps surface Docker sprawl, cache noise, and stale helper residue without touching protected Claude/Codex state. Happy to give you early access if you want to try it.

### Version 2: Curiosity Hook

> Quick one: how much storage do you think Docker and cache noise are quietly taking on your Mac right now? We built DreamCleanr to surface the reclaimable parts without guessing at protected state. Want the link?

### Version 3: Value First

> We have been giving a few AI/dev folks early access to DreamCleanr. The core idea is simple: visible receipts, Docker-aware cleanup, protected AI state, no fake optimizer hype. Want me to send it over?

## Creator Angles

| Angle | When to use it | Example title |
|---|---|---|
| Reclaim space | creator likes before/after results | `I cleaned my AI dev Mac without breaking Docker` |
| Trust-first | creator is skeptical of generic cleaners | `A Mac cleaner that actually shows what it touched` |
| AI workflow hygiene | creator covers AI coding tools | `Claude, Codex, and Docker can quietly wreck your disk` |
| GitHub-first credibility | creator cares about transparent tools | `This dev cleaner ships like a real open tool, not adware` |

## Approved Incentives

- free early access
- future lifetime Pro for a small number of high-signal testers
- manual affiliate or rev-share discussion later, only after the commercial model is real

Do not offer:

- payment for positive reviews
- claims of exclusive access to features that do not exist
- any automated affiliate program before the business model is implemented

## What To Send

- homepage link
- pricing page link
- one screenshot or sample receipt image
- one sentence on current shipped surfaces
- one sentence on what is planned next

## Strong creator asks

Ask for angles like:

- `I cleaned my AI-dev Mac and here is what happened`
- `Docker and cache noise are probably costing you more space than you think`
- `This tool shows what is safe to delete instead of guessing`

## Follow-Up Cadence

- initial message
- one follow-up after 5 to 7 days
- stop if there is no response

## Success Criteria

- creator replies with interest or feedback
- creator understands what DreamCleanr does today in under 30 seconds
- creator does not confuse planned detector work for current capabilities
